About Us

Adam & Bing, P.C. Is an experienced and client- oriented law firm. Our clients are numerous state and national banks, savings banks, mortgage companies, and other financial institutions. We represent our clients primarily in commercial and consumer lending.

Each of our attorneys strive to develop a long term, one-on-one relationship with the client. The firm conducts frequent seminars concerning commercial, real estate and compliance matters.

STEPHEN W. BING

He has been a practicing attorney for more than thirty two (32) years. Along with specializing in lender loan document preparation (home equity, SBA, residential, large commercial loans, etc.), he represents lenders in the area of foreclosure matters and collection matters. Additionally, he represented two title company fee offices for several years in variousreal estate title and closing matters. In addition to Texas, he is a member of the Colorado Bar.
